## 1. Usage Rules

- Every requirement must trace to a `DEC-*` entry; a decision may be marked `Verified` only after all acceptance criteria pass.
- Status values are restricted to `Not started`, `Partial`, `Blocked`, and `Verified`.
- An item must not be marked `Verified` without reproducible test evidence or PR/commit evidence.
- `Planned` test evidence identifies an intended artefact and does not mean that the test has passed.
- Owners and reviewers follow the Week 7 role allocation, and the reviewer must differ from the owner.

### Report Destination Key

- `Group — Requirements/Data Design`: requirements, data-contract, and design sections of the group report.
- `Group — Implementation/Testing`: implementation and testing sections of the group report.
- `Individual — Modelling/Evaluation`: modelling, filtering, and evaluation thread of the individual report.
- `Journal — Workflow/Provenance`: task-oriented workflow, reliability, and provenance evidence for the journal paper.

## 5. Week 7 Review Gate

The matrix may be frozen only when all of the following conditions are met:

1. Every decision from `DEC-01` through `DEC-25` is covered by at least one RTM row.
2. Owners and reviewers match the Week 7 role allocation.
3. Every acceptance criterion supports an objective pass/fail decision through an automated or reproducible manual test.
4. Every `Partial` item cites current evidence and states the remaining gap.
5. Subsequent issues link both the RTM ID and DEC ID; status changes to `Verified` only after PR/commit/test evidence is attached.
